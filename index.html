Overview

Facta Non Verba Academy was established in 2018 by Tshepo Chiwaya it is based in small township called Itsoseng in North West province

Mission

Our mission is to help all the learners in Itsoseng and the surrounding areas to improve their marks in subjects like Mathematics and Physical Sciences so they can be able to study their dream courses after high school "making education fashionable"
